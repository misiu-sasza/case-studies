The baseline
The account averaged a 0.3% click-to-conversion rate on broad list sends and a 46% open rate across roughly 70 campaign sends and 20 live flows in the year following June 2025. Each of the five emails below beat those numbers through a different mechanism: segmentation, urgency, narrative trust-building, visual proof, or layered design. That variety is the finding.

Takeaway

Format didn't decide the winner. Fit did.

A plain-text founder note outsold a designed product launch. A welcome-flow email outearned every campaign sent to the full list. All five matched their message to where the reader was: just subscribed, mid-consideration, three hours from a deadline, meeting a new product, or scrolling past a hundred other Halloween sales. The common thread is fit between message and moment. The program's job is diagnosing that.

Source: Klaviyo campaign and flow reporting, trailing 12 months from June 2025. Revenue figures reflect Klaviyo's Placed Order attribution model.
